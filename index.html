About Me

Full-Stack Web Developer and Musician with a particular interest in front-end application interaction and visualization along with back-end networking and data transference. My initial interest in code began through building music applications in my bachelors of Music Theory at the University of Georgia and wanted to bring my analytical and pattern-based approach to other kinds of applications. I completed the Coding Bootcamp at Georgia Tech with skills in Javascript, HTML, CSS, NodeJS, ReactJS, Express, and Sequelize with MySQL and Postgres, and mongoose with MongoDB. I also have experience with audio DSP frameworks like MaxMSP, SuperCollider, and PureData and with graphics libraries like p5.js, Three.js, and glslify. I believe that there is a wealth of creative solutions to every problem and collaboration through code is the most exciting way for groups to find those solutions.
